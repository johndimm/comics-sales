Privacy Policy – Comics Sales

Last updated: 2026-02-24

Comics Sales (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account and authentication information provided by eBay OAuth when you authorize the app.
  • Listing-related data needed to create and manage draft listings (title, description, price, category, and images).
  • Operational logs for debugging and reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To create, edit, and manage eBay draft listings on your behalf.
  • To upload listing photos and associated metadata required for listing workflows.
  • To maintain service reliability, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot errors.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We only share data with eBay APIs as required to provide listing functionality.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for listing operations, troubleshooting, and compliance with platform requirements.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect account tokens and listing data.

Your Choices

  • You may revoke app access at any time in your eBay account permissions.
  • You may request deletion of locally stored operational data by contacting us.
